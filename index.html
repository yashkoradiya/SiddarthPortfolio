About me

PHOTOGRAPHER

Siddarth is a dedicated wildlife conservationist and drone enthusiast. From a young age, his fascination with drones led him to develop an interest in engineering. Siddarth has built several drones on his own and honed his skills through online resources. However, a photo tour that he took during the COVID-19 lockdown changed his life's course. While editing documentaries, he discovered the harsh realities that endangered species face daily. This encounter inspired him to pursue wildlife conservation and protect these animals. Siddarth started working as a video editor for wildlife documentaries and later became a drone pilot, which improved his access to wildlife and allowed him to capture stunning footage. He has completed six assignments for Karnataka Forest Department and worked on various conservation-related projects, including collaborations with Green Oscar Award winner Prakash Mattada. His ultimate goal is to share the hidden stories of the jungle with the world and make a difference in preserving wildlife.
